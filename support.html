- Have any Questions?

This is Warmth Zone Portable Baby Incubator Support. There are some frequent questions and answers below. Scroll down to check the questions and answers. If you have any other questions, please email us and we will help you.

Q: How do I reuse the PCMs after I used them?

A: You will have to put the used PCMs into boiling water. After a while, the PCM will turn again into liquid. Then, you can use them again.

Q: How does the PCM work?

A: PCM stands for Phase Change Material and PCM absorbs or releases large amounts of energy or heat. This heats the baby's body temperature and keeps the baby's body warm.

Q: Does the time outside the healthy range matter?

A: Yes, the baby's time outside the healthy range matters a lot. The less time outside the healthy range keeps the baby healthier. Also, this depends on what PCMs that we are going to use.
